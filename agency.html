About Sofia

I'm a Film & Television Production and Advertising student at Boston University, building work that sits between concept, production, and platform. On the agency side, I like ideas that can be said simply, produced cleanly, and adapted to where people actually watch.

My production background makes me useful in creative rooms because I think about the idea and the execution at the same time: what the spot is saying, what it needs on set, how it will edit, and whether the final deliverable fits the platform.
