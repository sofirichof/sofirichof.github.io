About Sofia

I'm a film and television producer finishing a BS in Film & Television Production at Boston University — double major in Advertising, graduating August 2026. I've produced SAG-AFTRA short films, worked as a segment producer at Telemundo 52, gone on-set in Santiago, Chile for a live variety show, and spent the last year producing three seasons of Baystate TV Drama.

I'm bilingual in English and Spanish (working French too), which has shaped both the work I gravitate toward and the stories I want to tell.
