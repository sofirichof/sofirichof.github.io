Live With TY — Season 1 Intro
Project Overview

Live With TY was part of a live television workflow where timing, energy, and clarity had to carry from production into the final edit. As Chief Editor, I helped shape the finished episode and contributed to the post-production quality of a program recognized by NATAS.

What I Did
Delivered 10 video packages per week, formatted for ESPN, YouTube, and social media across the full season.
Led a team of 4 editors, coordinating workflow and maintaining consistency across all weekly deliverables.
Designed and built the show's graphics package and intro sequence from scratch.
Collaborated with producers on publishing strategy and episode pacing across multiple platforms.
Won a NATAS University Sports Program Award for S1 Ep. 11 — the episode that defined the season's editorial voice.
